Newslizr






What is Newslizr ?

  1. Prioritizes news according to your interest
  2. Includes a hint of general news as well because too much personalization is bad
  3. Tunes itself based on your feedback

How does it work ?

  1. Login with you facebook, twitter or google+ account
  2. Link other social networking accounts
  3. Newslizr takes this as the dataset to determine your interest

Part 2

  1. Index all news source sites like nytimes.com, foxnews.com etc using apache nutch + apache solr
  2. Use a feature vector such that more weightage is given to the user interest
  3. Sort the results on the basis of the highest score and push it to the user feed
